Oden Recipes

Description

I love the savoury, rich taste of beef in black bean sauce, Although it takes a little bit more effort to make than other stir fry recipes (especially when you're making your own black bean sauce), it's actually very simple. The sauce can be made ahead, or you can make it as part of this recipe (apart from the hour needed to soak the beans, the rest of the sauce comes together in minutes).

Ingredients

  • oil
  • stock
  • pepper
  • black beans
  • sugar
  • rice vinegar
  • garlic
  • cornflour
  • ginger
  • rice wine
  • sesame oil
  • soy sauce

Steps (for sauce)

  1. The black bean sauce is made by first soaking fermented black beans fermented black beans in water for an hour.
  2. Then we rinse and drain the beans and give them a bit of a mush up with a fork.
  3. Next we fry up garlic, ginger and the black beans in a little sesame oil and neutral oil.
  4. Add in rice wine, soy sauce, rice vinegar, sugar, pepper and stock and bring to the boil.
  5. Simmer the sauce for 5 minutes, before stirring in a little cornstarch slurry to thicken the sauce.
  6. That sauce is now ready to use.
